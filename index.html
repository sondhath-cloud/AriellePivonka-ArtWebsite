The Abstract Emotions Series

This series was born from a period of deep introspection and emotional exploration. Each piece represents a different emotional state, translated through color, form, and texture.

The process begins with meditation and emotional awareness, allowing the feelings to guide the choice of colors and brushstrokes. The result is a collection that speaks to the universal human experience of emotion.

These works invite viewers to connect with their own emotional landscapes, creating a dialogue between the artist's expression and the viewer's interpretation.

The Urban Reflections Series

Living in the city has always fascinated me with its constant movement, energy, and complexity. This series captures the essence of urban life through the medium of charcoal and ink.

Each piece is created during different times of day, capturing the changing moods of the cityscape. From the quiet moments of dawn to the bustling energy of rush hour.

The contrast between light and shadow, movement and stillness, creates a visual narrative that reflects the human experience within the urban environment.

The Mixed Explorations Series

This series represents my journey into experimental art-making, combining traditional and unconventional materials to create unique textures and visual experiences.

Each piece incorporates found objects, natural materials, and various painting techniques, creating layers of meaning and visual interest that invite closer inspection.

The process is often unpredictable, allowing the materials themselves to guide the creative direction and reveal unexpected beauty in the combination of elements.

About the Artist

Arielle Pivonka is a contemporary artist whose work explores the complex relationship between emotion, memory, and visual expression. With a background in fine arts and a passion for experimentation, Arielle creates pieces that invite viewers to engage with their own inner landscapes.

Her work spans multiple mediums, from traditional oil paintings to experimental mixed-media pieces, each telling a unique story of human experience and artistic discovery.
